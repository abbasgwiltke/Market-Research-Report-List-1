Flat heaters are designed for applications requiring even heat distribution across a surface, making them ideal for processes like semiconductor manufacturing and die bonding. Cylindrical heaters, on the other hand, are tailored for tubular applications, providing efficient heating in confined spaces such as reactors and pipelines. Both heater types leverage AlN's excellent thermal conductivity and electrical insulation properties for improved performance and reliability.</p></p>
