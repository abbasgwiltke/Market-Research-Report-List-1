<p><p>The power-operated machine tool chucks market is categorized primarily into 2-jaw, 3-jaw, and 4-jaw chucks, each serving distinct applications. 2-jaw chucks excel in gripping irregularly shaped workpieces, ensuring precise alignment. 3-jaw chucks are versatile and widely used for cylindrical shapes due to their self-centering capabilities, making them ideal for routine machining tasks. 4-jaw chucks offer enhanced flexibility, allowing for clamping of square and asymmetrical objects, thus providing more accurate positioning and stability during complex operations.</p></p>
