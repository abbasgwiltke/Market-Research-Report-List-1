<p><p>The bare copper tape market is categorized into three main types based on the hardness of the material: annealed (soft), half-hard, and hard. Annealed copper tape is flexible and easily shaped, making it suitable for applications that require bending and intricate installations. Half-hard copper tape offers a balance between flexibility and strength, ideal for general wiring applications. Hard copper tape is rigid and durable, often used in high-stress environments, providing excellent conductivity and structural integrity. Each type serves distinct industrial and electrical needs.</p></p>
