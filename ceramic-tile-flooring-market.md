<p><ul><li>Glazed</li><li>Unglazed</li><li>Lapped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic tile flooring is categorized into three main types: glazed, unglazed, and lapped. Glazed tiles feature a glass-like coating that enhances durability and offers a wide range of colors and designs, making them suitable for various aesthetics. Unglazed tiles are naturally porous and provide a more rustic look, ideal for areas with high foot traffic due to their slip-resistant nature. Lapped tiles combine both glazed and unglazed surfaces, offering unique visual effects while maintaining durability, making them versatile for diverse applications.</p></p>
