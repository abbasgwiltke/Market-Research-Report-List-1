<p><strong>The Leaf Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Leaf Spring</li><li>Mono-Leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leaf spring market can be categorized into two main types: multi-leaf and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple layers of metal that provide enhanced load-bearing capacity and improved durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, mono-leaf springs feature a single, robust layer designed for lighter applications, offering flexibility and weight savings. Both types cater to different vehicle requirements, balancing performance, cost, and weight, thereby playing vital roles in suspension systems across various automotive sectors.</p></p>
