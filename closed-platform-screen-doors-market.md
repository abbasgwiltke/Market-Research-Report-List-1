<p><p>The Closed Platform Screen Doors (PSD) market consists of two main types: Full-Closed Type and Semi-Closed Type. Full-Closed Type doors provide complete separation between the platform and the train, enhancing safety by preventing accidental falls and improving energy efficiency by controlling air flow. Semi-Closed Type doors, while still offering some barrier, allow for partial openings, facilitating passenger access and ventilation. Both types aim to improve safety and operational efficiency in transit systems, catering to varying urban infrastructural needs.</p></p>
