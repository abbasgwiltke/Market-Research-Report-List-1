<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anticorrosive wood lacquers market is primarily divided into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based lacquers utilize a water solvent, offering low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them environmentally friendly and easier to clean. They dry quickly and are less odorous. In contrast, solvent-based lacquers contain organic solvents, providing a durable finish with excellent adhesion and resistance to harsh conditions. However, they emit higher VOC levels and require more care during application. Each type caters to different user preferences and environmental regulations.</p></p>
