<p><p>The New Material Cutter market is categorized into three main types: cemented carbide, ceramic, and superhard materials. Cemented carbide cutters are known for their durability and resistance to wear, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Ceramic cutters offer excellent hardness and thermal stability, suitable for machining at high speeds. Superhard materials, such as cubic boron nitride and synthetic diamond, provide exceptional cutting efficiency and precision for challenging materials. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and enhances machining capabilities.</p></p>
