<p><p>Thermal cycling thermoelectric coolers (TECs) are devices that use the Peltier effect to achieve temperature control, often utilized in laboratory and industrial settings. The market comprises two main types: single-stage thermal cycling TECs, which provide moderate cooling and stability for simpler applications, and multistage thermal cycling TECs, designed for more demanding environments requiring greater temperature differentials and precise control. Both types cater to diverse applications, including electronics cooling, biomedical devices, and temperature management in scientific research.</p></p>
