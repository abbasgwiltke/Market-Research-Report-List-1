<p><p>The LPG tanker market is segmented into various types based on size and capacity. VLGC (Very Large Gas Carrier) typically transports large volumes of liquefied petroleum gas, making it ideal for long-haul routes. LGC (Large Gas Carrier) is slightly smaller, suitable for regional trades. MGC (Medium Gas Carrier) serves mid-sized shipments, often used for niche markets. SGC (Small Gas Carrier) is designed for smaller ports and coastal deliveries, focusing on flexibility for short distances and smaller quantities.</p></p>
