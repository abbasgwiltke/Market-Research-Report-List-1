<p><p>Glass Ionomer Cement (GIC) is a dental material used for fillings, categorized into two main types based on curing mechanisms: Self-light Curing Type and Dual Curing Type. Self-light Curing Type sets through a chemical reaction, making it easy to manipulate and ideal for moisture-rich environments. On the other hand, Dual Curing Type combines both chemical and light activation, offering versatility and enhancing strength, allowing it to be used in various clinical situations. Both types provide strong adhesion to dental tissues.</p></p>
