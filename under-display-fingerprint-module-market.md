<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The under-display fingerprint module market comprises three primary types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Capacitive modules use electrical signals to detect fingerprint ridges, offering quick and accurate recognition. Optical modules capture high-resolution images of fingerprints, providing effective performance in low-light conditions. Ultrasonic modules utilize sound waves to create detailed 3D fingerprint maps, enhancing security and functionality. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to varying consumer preferences and device requirements in the growing market for biometric authentication solutions.</p></p>
