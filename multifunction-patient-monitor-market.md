<p><ul><li>Electrocardiogram (ECG) Devices</li><li>Event Monitors</li><li>Implantable Loop Recorders (ILR)</li><li>Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) Devices</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifunction patient monitor market encompasses various devices designed for comprehensive patient monitoring. Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices capture heart activity, while event monitors record sporadic cardiac events, allowing for extended monitoring. Implantable loop recorders (ILRs) provide continuous cardiac data for long periods, useful in diagnosing irregular heart rhythms. Cardiac output monitoring (COM) devices assess the heart's efficiency in pumping blood. Other types in this market may include tools for monitoring oxygen saturation, blood pressure, and respiratory rates, enhancing overall patient care.</p></p>
