<p><strong>The Biosolids Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerobic Digestion</li><li>Anaerobic Digestion</li><li>Nutrient Removal or Recovery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biosolids management encompasses various market types, primarily including aerobic digestion, anaerobic digestion, and nutrient removal or recovery. Aerobic digestion involves the use of oxygen to break down organic materials, promoting effective stabilization and reducing pathogens. Anaerobic digestion occurs in the absence of oxygen, facilitating biogas production for energy recovery while reducing waste volume. Nutrient removal or recovery focuses on extracting valuable nutrients, such as nitrogen and phosphorus, from wastewater to minimize environmental impact and support sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
