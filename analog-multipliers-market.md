<p><p>Analog multipliers are categorized based on their operational quadrants. </p><p>**Single Quadrant Multipliers** operate in the first quadrant, handling positive inputs and producing positive outputs. </p><p>**Two Quadrant Multipliers** can process both positive and negative inputs, resulting in positive and negative outputs, suitable for applications requiring mixed signals. </p><p>**Four Quadrant Multipliers** handle all combinations of positive and negative inputs and outputs, allowing for more complex signal processing. These multipliers are essential in various applications, including audio processing and communications.</p></p>
