<p><p>The Amaranth Oil market consists of three main extraction methods: Cold Pressed, Supercritical CO2 Extraction, and Organic Solvent (Hexane) Extraction. Cold Pressed involves mechanically pressing seeds to preserve nutrients without heat, yielding high-quality oil. Supercritical CO2 Extraction uses carbon dioxide at high pressure, offering a pure product by selectively extracting desired compounds. Organic Solvent Extraction employs chemical solvents like hexane, efficiently extracting oil but potentially introducing residues. Each method impacts oil purity, quality, and market appeal.</p></p>
