<p><p>The trim systems market for marine applications primarily includes trim tabs and interceptors. Interceptors are fixed, often more robust devices that alter the water flow to improve performance and maneuverability without moving parts. Both systems play crucial roles in optimizing vessel performance, ensuring smoother rides, and enhancing fuel economy, catering to various vessel types in recreational and commercial marine sectors.</p></p>
