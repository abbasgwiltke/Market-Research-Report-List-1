<p><p>The metallic sealing gasket market is segmented into two primary types: round and non-circular gaskets. Round gaskets, typically used in standard flanged connections, provide uniform sealing surfaces, ensuring effective pressure retention. Non-circular gaskets come in various shapes, such as square, rectangular, or custom designs, catering to specific applications where round gaskets are unsuitable. Both types are critical in industries like oil and gas, aerospace, and manufacturing, where reliability and durability under extreme conditions are essential for maintaining system integrity.</p></p>
