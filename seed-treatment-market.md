<p><p>The seed treatment market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Chemical seed treatment**, which involves applying chemical pesticides or fungicides to seeds to protect them from pathogens and pests, enhancing germination and crop yield.</p><p>2. **Biological seed treatment** utilizes natural organisms, such as bacteria or fungi, to promote seed health and resist diseases, focusing on sustainable agriculture practices.</p><p>3. **Physical seed treatment** employs non-chemical methods like heat, cold, or soaking to improve seed quality and viability, ensuring better performance during planting.</p></p>
