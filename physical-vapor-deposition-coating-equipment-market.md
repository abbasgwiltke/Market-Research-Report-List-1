<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) coating equipment market is segmented into several types, including sputtering deposition and evaporation deposition. Sputtering deposition involves ejecting particles from a target material onto a substrate, creating a thin film. Evaporation deposition, on the other hand, uses thermal energy to vaporize material, which then condenses on the substrate. Other methods may include laser ablation or ion plating. Each technique offers distinct advantages for applications in electronics, optics, and protective coatings, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
