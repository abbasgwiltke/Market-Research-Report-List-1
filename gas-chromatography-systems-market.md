<p><strong>The Gas Chromatography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-solid Chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid Chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas chromatography systems are essential analytical tools used to separate and analyze volatile compounds. There are two primary types: Gas-solid chromatography (GSC) involves a stationary solid phase, enabling the separation of gases based on adsorption properties. It is effective for analyzing polar compounds. In contrast, Gas-liquid chromatography (GLC) utilizes a liquid stationary phase coated on a solid support, adept at separating non-polar compounds. GLC is widely used in environmental monitoring, food quality control, and petrochemical analysis. Both techniques are crucial in various industrial applications.</p></p>
