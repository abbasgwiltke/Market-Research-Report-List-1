<p><strong>The Manual Lensmeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visual Type Lensmeter</li><li>Projection Type Lensmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual lensmeter market consists of two primary types: visual type lensmeters and projection type lensmeters. Visual type lensmeters require the operator to manually align lenses and read measurements directly through an eyepiece, making them suitable for precise assessments in various optical applications. In contrast, projection type lensmeters utilize a light source to project an image of the lens onto a screen, allowing for easier readings and improved accuracy, catering to high-volume environments such as optical labs and clinics.</p></p>
