<p><p>The nasal packing device market comprises two main types: bioabsorbable devices and biological nonabsorbent devices. Bioabsorbable devices are designed to dissolve naturally in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal and reducing patient discomfort. In contrast, biological nonabsorbent devices are made from inert materials that remain in place until removed, providing structural support and reducing bleeding. Both types cater to varying patient needs and preferences in procedures like sinus surgery and nasal trauma management.</p></p>
