<p><strong>The Light Electric Motorcycle Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Central Motor"</li><li>"Hub Motor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the light electric motorcycle market, two primary motor types are prominent: central motors and hub motors. Central motors are typically located near the bike's center, providing balanced weight distribution and better handling. They often offer higher torque and efficiency, appealing to performance-oriented riders. On the other hand, hub motors are integrated into the wheel hub, simplifying design and maintenance. This configuration allows for a quieter ride and frees up space in the frame, making them ideal for urban commuting and recreational use.</p></p>
