<p><p>The rotary angle sensors market comprises several key types. Rotary potentiometers measure angular position using resistance changes, while resolvers function as electrical devices that convert rotary motion into an electrical signal. Rotary encoders detect angular position and motion, often used for precise control in automation. Rotary Variable Differential Transformers (RVDTs) utilize electromagnetic principles to measure angular displacement, providing high accuracy and reliability. Each type serves diverse applications across automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics sectors, enhancing performance and functionality.</p></p>
