<p><p>The hemostats and tissue sealants market comprises two main types: hemostats and tissue sealants. Hemostats are products used to control bleeding by promoting clotting, often employed during surgical procedures or trauma care. They include agents such as gelatin sponges, collagen, and thrombin. Tissue sealants, on the other hand, are used to adhere tissues together and facilitate healing, typically used in surgical settings to close wounds or incisions. Both types are essential for improving surgical outcomes and patient recovery.</p></p>
