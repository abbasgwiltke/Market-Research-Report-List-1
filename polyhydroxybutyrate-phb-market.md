<p><p>Polyhydroxybutyrate (PHB) is a biodegradable polymer produced by microorganisms as an energy source. The PHB market primarily includes types such as PHB, PHBV (polyhydroxybutyrate-co-valerate), and PHBHx (polyhydroxybutyrate-co-hydroxyhexanoate). PHB is known for its rigidity and thermal stability, while PHBV offers enhanced flexibility and improved degradation properties by incorporating valerate. PHBHx, which includes hydroxyhexanoate, further enhances elasticity and biodegradability. Together, these variants cater to diverse applications in packaging, agriculture, and biomedical fields.</p></p>
