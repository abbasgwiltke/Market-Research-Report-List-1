<p><p>Data encryption software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises encryption involves software installed and operated locally on a company’s servers, providing direct control over sensitive data. This option is preferred by organizations with stringent security requirements. Conversely, cloud encryption software is hosted on third-party platforms, offering scalability and ease of access while enabling efficient management of encryption keys and integration with other cloud services. Both options serve to protect data from unauthorized access.</p></p>
