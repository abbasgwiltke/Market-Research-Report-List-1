<p><ul><li>PET Scanners (Only Including PET)</li><li>PET-CT Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET scanners market is categorized into two main types: standalone PET scanners and PET-CT scanners. Standalone PET scanners are specialized devices that provide detailed images of metabolic activity in the body, primarily used for cancer diagnosis and monitoring. In contrast, PET-CT scanners combine positron emission tomography and computed tomography, offering both metabolic and anatomical imaging, which enhances diagnostic accuracy. The dual functionality of PET-CT scanners makes them increasingly popular in clinical settings for comprehensive evaluations.</p></p>
